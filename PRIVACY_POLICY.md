# Nyx Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

Nyx ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service" or "App").

By using Nyx, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Nyx uses a zero-knowledge architecture, which means:

- **We never have access to your files, PIN, or encryption keys**
- All encryption and decryption happens on your device
- Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device
- We cannot decrypt or access your files, even if requested by law enforcement
- If you forget your PIN, we cannot recover your data

This is a fundamental security principle of Nyx - we designed it so that even we cannot access your data.

- We only receive confirmation of your subscription status

### App Usage Data
- We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (e.g., crash reports, feature usage)
- This data cannot be linked to your identity or files

### Device Information

- This information is used solely for app functionality and troubleshooting

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Your files or media content
- Your PIN or passwords
- Your encryption keys
- Personal information beyond what's necessary for app functionality
- Location data
- Contact information
- Browsing history or web activity

## Data Storage

All your files are stored locally on your device in an encrypted format. We do not:

- Store your files on our servers
- Sync your files to cloud services
- Access your device's storage beyond what's necessary for app functionality
- Share your data with third parties
- Transmit your files over the internet (except for local WiFi transfer feature)

Your encrypted files remain on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

## Security Measures

Nyx implements multiple layers of security:

- **AES-256-GCM Encryption**: Military-grade encryption for all files
- **Per-File Encryption Keys**: Each file has a unique encryption key derived from your master key
- **On-Device Encryption**: All encryption happens locally on your device
- **PIN Protection**: Your master key is derived from your PIN using iterative HMAC-SHA256 (100,000 iterations)
- **Secure Storage**: Sensitive data (PIN hashes, encryption keys) stored in iOS Keychain/Android Keystore
- **Calculator Disguise**: App appears as a calculator for privacy
- **Decoy Vault**: Premium users can create a fake vault with a different PIN
- **Tamper Detection**: Detects debugger, root access, and failed unlock attempts

Despite these measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we implement industry-standard security practices.

- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, though due to our zero-knowledge architecture, we cannot provide access to encrypted files even if legally compelled.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access your data**: All your data is stored on your device - you have full access
- **Delete your data**: You can delete files individually or wipe all vault data at any time
- **Control your data**: You decide what files to store in the vault
- **Export your data**: You can download files from the vault at any time
- **Uninstall**: You can uninstall the app at any time, which removes all app data from your device

Since we don't store your data on our servers, you have full control over your information.

## Data Retention

- **Subscription Information**: Retained by Apple/Google according to their policies
