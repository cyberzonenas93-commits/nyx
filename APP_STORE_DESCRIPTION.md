## App Store Guidelines Compliance Notes

**Post–review updates reflected in description:**
- **Background audio**: Described as “background audio playback” for playing vault audio when the app is minimised or from the lock screen/Control Center (Guideline 2.5.4 – legitimate media player use).
- **Photos API**: No claim that the app deletes or removes content from the user’s photo library. Explicit line added: “Files you export to your photo library remain there; the app does not delete or modify your device gallery” (Guideline 2.5.1 – no PhotoManager deletion).
- **Calculator disguise**: Removed. All references to a calculator interface or app disguise have been removed from the description, promotional text, keywords, and What's New.

**Compliant language used:**
- "Privacy vault" (not "hide from spouse/parents")
- "Protect sensitive documents" (legitimate use case)
- "Organize important files" (legitimate use case)
- "Secure backups" (legitimate use case)
- "Personal files" (not "secret files")
- Focus on user control and data protection

**Avoided language:**
- No mention of hiding content from family members
- No mention of bypassing security or authorities
- No mention of "secret" or "hidden" in negative context
- No suggestion of nefarious use cases

**Legitimate use cases highlighted:**
- Protecting sensitive documents
- Organizing important files
- Creating secure backups
- Separating private files from main gallery
- Transferring files securely between devices

This description reflects post–App Store review changes and emphasizes legitimate privacy and security features while avoiding any language that might suggest inappropriate use cases.
