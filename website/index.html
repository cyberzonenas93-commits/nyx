Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my vault?

Double-tap anywhere on the calculator screen or long-press the "=" button to unlock your vault.

What if I forget my PIN?

If you forget your PIN, Nyx does not offer an account-based recovery flow. Keep your unlock method somewhere safe before relying on the app for important files.

How do I upgrade to Unlimited?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Manage Subscription" to upgrade to unlimited storage.

Is my data secure?

Nyx keeps vault files local to your device and protects app access with your PIN or pattern, secure credential storage, and automatic locking. Nyx does not upload vault contents to its own servers.

What file types are supported?

Nyx supports photos, videos, and documents including PDF, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), Word (.docx, .doc), and Pages files.

Can I use both PIN and Pattern unlock?

You can choose either PIN or Pattern unlock when setting up your vault. You can switch between them in Settings > Security.

What are multiple vaults?

Premium users can create separate vault spaces with their own trigger codes and unlock credentials so different groups of files stay isolated inside the app.

Are original files deleted after import?

No. Nyx imports a copy into the vault. After you confirm the import worked, you can remove the original yourself if you want it gone from your regular library or files app.

Privacy & Security

Nyx is designed as a local-first vault: files stay on your device, and Nyx does not operate a hosted cloud for your vault contents.
