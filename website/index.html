Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my vault?

Double-tap anywhere on the calculator screen or long-press the "=" button to unlock your vault.

What if I forget my PIN?

Unfortunately, if you forget your PIN, your data cannot be recovered due to zero-knowledge encryption. This is a security feature that ensures your privacy. Please store your PIN securely.

How do I upgrade to Unlimited?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Manage Subscription" to upgrade to unlimited storage.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption. We use zero-knowledge architecture, meaning we never see your files, passwords, or encryption keys. Your privacy is our priority.

What file types are supported?

Nyx supports photos, videos, and documents including PDF, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), Word (.docx, .doc), and Pages files.

Can I use both PIN and Pattern unlock?

You can choose either PIN or Pattern unlock when setting up your vault. You can switch between them in Settings > Security.

What is the Decoy Vault?

The Decoy Vault is a premium feature that allows you to set a fake passcode. If entered, it shows an empty vault, providing an extra layer of privacy protection.

Are original files deleted after import?

Yes. Once your files are encrypted and stored in the vault, the original files are automatically deleted from your device for security.

Privacy & Security

Nyx uses zero-knowledge encryption, meaning your data is encrypted on your device and we cannot access it even if we wanted to. Your privacy is truly yours.
