Nyx Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-09

Introduction

Nyx (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Nyx mobile application (the “Service”).

What we collect

Nyx is designed to minimize data collection. We may collect:

  • Subscription information: purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not store your payment details.
  • Diagnostics: crash reports and basic, anonymized usage data to improve stability and performance.
  • Device/app info: device type, OS version, and app version for support and compatibility.

We do not collect your vault media contents, PIN, passwords, or encryption keys.

How your files are handled

Your vault files are stored locally on your device. Nyx does not upload your vault files to our servers. Any file transfer features (such as WiFi Transfer) are designed to operate over your local network.

Permissions

Nyx may request permissions to enable features you choose to use, such as:

  • Photos: to import media into the vault and export media back to your library when you choose.
  • Camera: to capture photos/videos for the vault.
  • Face ID: to authenticate and unlock the vault.
  • Local Network: to enable WiFi Transfer between your device and a computer on the same WiFi.

Third‑party services

Nyx uses Apple App Store services for subscriptions and app distribution. Any payment processing is handled by Apple.

Data retention

  • Your files: remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.
  • Subscription records: retained by Apple according to their policies.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@nyx.app
